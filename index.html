About Friends Tuition Centre

Our Campus
10+ Years of Excellence

Quality Education Since 2014

Friends Tuition Centre provides quality coaching for LKG to +2 students with individual attention, regular assessments, exam preparation, homework support, and academic excellence. We believe every student has the potential to excel with the right guidance and support.

Our experienced faculty members are dedicated to nurturing young minds through personalized teaching methods. We offer both Tamil and English medium instruction, ensuring students can learn in their preferred language while maintaining high academic standards.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We provide coaching for all classes from LKG to +2 (Higher Secondary). This includes primary school (1st-5th), middle school (6th-8th), high school (9th-10th), and higher secondary (11th-12th) for both State Board and CBSE syllabus.

Yes, we provide coaching in both Tamil and English medium. Students can choose their preferred language of instruction, and our experienced faculty are proficient in teaching in both languages.

We offer affordable fee structures that vary based on the class and subjects. Please contact us directly or visit our centre to get detailed information about fees. We believe in providing quality education at reasonable prices.

We conduct weekly tests and monthly assessments to track student progress. Regular parent-teacher meetings are held to provide feedback and discuss the student's performance. We also maintain detailed progress reports for each student.

We have flexible batch timings to accommodate school schedules. Morning and evening batches are available. Please contact us to know the specific timings for your preferred class and subject combination.

Yes, we provide complete homework support. Teachers help students understand and complete their school homework. We also provide additional practice worksheets to reinforce learning concepts.

You can enroll your child by visiting our centre directly at Chennai Main Road, Mundiyampakkam, Viluppuram. Alternatively, you can call us at 8056541102 or 9894269392, or send us a WhatsApp enquiry through this website.
